About Us

A little about who we are and what we do.

ExZistence is the official Electrical Engineering student club at University of Prince Mugrin (UPM), Located at Al-Madinah, Saudi Arabia. We're a proud community of students passionate about electronics, control systems, power engineering, telecommunications, and everything in between.

We run workshops, technical talks, project showcases, and competitions throughout the year — giving members hands-on experience beyond the classroom. Whether you're soldering your first circuit or looking to connect with professionals, there's a place for you here.
